About UDBHAV'26

50 teams. 10 problems. 24 hours. One winner.

01
Overview
🚀
⚡

UDBHAV'26 is a high-intensity, 24-hour hackathon hosted at Sage University, Indore, bringing together top student innovators to build real-world solutions under pressure, where creativity, speed, and execution define success.

02
Scale & Participation
👥
🏆

The event features 50 carefully shortlisted teams and over 200 participants, all competing in a fast-paced environment designed to push technical and problem-solving limits.

50Teams
200+Participants
24hNon-Stop
03
Challenge Format
💡
🎯

Participants will face 10 real-world problem statements released in a rapid 15-minute PS drop window, requiring quick thinking, sharp decision-making, and efficient collaboration.

04
Why Participate
🤝
🌐

UDBHAV'26 offers hands-on exposure to industry-level challenges, opportunities to collaborate with talented peers, connect with mentors and judges, and gain recognition while competing for exciting rewards.

🏆 Prizes 🤝 Mentors 🌐 Network 💡 Real Challenges 🎯 Recognition
